Who We Are

Founded in 2020 by Roy Osherove and Shauli Rejwan, Web3 Developers Underground is Israel's largest developer-led community for blockchain and decentralized-technology enthusiasts.

We unite over 12,000 members online (3,200+ active in Israel) — engineers, founders, investors, academics and other Web3 practitioners — around a shared passion for building the future of decentralized systems.

Our Mission

Connect — ambitious developers and startup teams across Israel's Web3 landscape.

Educate — through deep-dive technical content, hands-on workshops and expert Q&A sessions.

Collaborate — on open-source sprints, hackathons and real-world projects that push the boundaries of blockchain technology.

“

Guided by Roy and Shauli's developer-first vision, we strive to propel both individual careers and the broader ecosystem forward — professionally, commercially and socially.”

Community Values

Knowledge Sharing

We believe in the power of open knowledge exchange. Our community is built on the idea that the best way to grow is by learning from each other. Whether you're a blockchain veteran or just starting out, everyone has something valuable to contribute.

Inclusion and Diversity

Web3 Developers Underground is committed to creating a welcoming space for everyone. We actively seek to include diverse voices and perspectives, believing that the future of decentralized technology should be built by a community that represents all people.

Innovation Through Collaboration

The most groundbreaking ideas emerge when brilliant minds come together. We foster an environment where collaboration is encouraged, where projects can find team members, and where innovative solutions to challenging problems are developed collectively.
